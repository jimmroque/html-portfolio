About Jimuell

I was born in the Philippines and moved to Canada at the age of 14 in July 2014. After completing my education at New Westminster Secondary School, I began my career as a chef, honing my skills in the culinary arts. Over time, I transitioned into the healthcare field, becoming a licensed optician and contact lens fitter.

Now, I'm embarking on a new journey, exploring the world of web development—a passion I discovered early in life but temporarily set aside while pursuing other career paths. Coding has always held a special place in my heart, and I’m excited to return to it, eager to combine my diverse experiences with my love for technology.
